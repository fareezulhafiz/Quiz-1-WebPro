Tourist Spots

Seremban offers diverse attractions, including Palm Mall for shopping and dining, the historic first French missionary church, and scenic hiking spots. Visitors can explore Malaysia’s first ostrich farm, enjoy a picturesque waterfall for picnics, and admire a mosque that blends modern and traditional Minangkabau architecture.

Palm Mall Seremban

Integrated with the Palm Seremban Hotel, Palm Mall is one of Seremban's largest lifestyle shopping complexes. It offers a variety of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities, catering to both locals and visitors. The mall features modern architecture, a spacious layout, and hosts various events, making it a vibrant hub for shopping and leisure.

Church of the Visitation

The first French missionary church of Seremban, inspired by Neo-Catholic architecture, features grand designs and intricate details like tall spires and stained glass windows. It stands as a significant cultural and historical landmark, reflecting the legacy of French missionaries in Malaysia.

Galeri Diraja Tuanku Jaafar

A gallery dedicated to the late Grand Ruler of Malaysia provides insights into his life and legacy through a collection of personal artifacts, photographs, and documents. Exhibits highlight his contributions to the nation and interactions with global leaders, while interactive displays enhance the experience. This space serves as both an educational resource and a tribute to his enduring influence on Malaysian history and culture.

Broga Hill

A famous hiking spot, this destination offers stunning views of sunrise and sunset, making it a favorite for outdoor enthusiasts. The trail meanders through lush landscapes, providing breathtaking panoramas of surrounding hills and valleys. Early risers can enjoy the serene sunrise, while sunset hikers witness a beautiful array of colors in the sky.

Jelita Ostrich Farm

Malaysia’s first ostrich farm is a unique attraction featuring ostriches and a variety of other animals. Visitors can enjoy guided tours, observing and feeding the ostriches while learning about their care and breeding.

Seremban Lake Garden

A beautiful spot surrounded by serene lakes and lush gardens, this destination is popular for joggers and family outings. Scenic walking and jogging paths wind through vibrant greenery, while families can enjoy picnics and playgrounds.

Masjid Negeri

A stunning mosque known for its distinctive architecture, blending modern design with traditional Minangkabau elements. It serves as a central place of worship and a prominent symbol of Seremban’s rich cultural heritage.

Ulu Bendul Waterfall

A scenic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, this destination is ideal for picnics and nature walks. The cascading water creates a tranquil atmosphere, while well-maintained trails invite exploration of the vibrant ecosystem. Families can enjoy leisurely picnics on grassy areas, all set against the soothing sounds of the waterfall. This picturesque spot offers great opportunities for photography and serves as a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Centipede Temple

Known for its iconic centipede statue, this attraction requires climbing 264 steps to reach the summit. As visitors ascend, they enjoy stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The statue, a striking symbol of cultural significance, serves as a great photo opportunity. The path features lush greenery and informative displays about the local ecosystem. At the top, guests are rewarded with panoramic vistas and a sense of accomplishment, making it a popular destination for hikers and outdoor enthusiasts.
