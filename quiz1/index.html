Seremban: The Heart

of Negeri Sembilan

Hello! I’m proud to call Seremban my hometown, a place where tradition meets modern life. Nestled in the lush landscapes of Negeri Sembilan, Seremban is known for its delicious local dishes, vibrant markets, and warm community spirit.

From savoring mouthwatering 'Cendol' and 'Masak Lemak Cili Api' to exploring the beautiful parks and historical sites, there’s so much to love about this city. Whether I’m enjoying a leisurely stroll in a park or indulging in local delicacies, Seremban always feels like home.

Join me as I share my experiences, celebrate our culture, and showcase the hidden gems of Seremban. Welcome to my Seremban – a place close to my heart!
